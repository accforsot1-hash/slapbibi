About Slap Bibi
Slap Bibi is a Solana meme coin with a mission. While most meme coins are pure speculation, $SLAPBIBI turns every trade into an act of solidarity with the people of Gaza.
Every time someone buys or sells $SLAPBIBI on pump.fun, a creator fee is generated in SOL. 25% of all accumulated fees are sent as SOL to the World Food Programme Gaza emergency fund. Every transfer is posted publicly on our X account with the transaction ID — fully verifiable on Solana Explorer.
The slap counter on this site is symbolic — it represents the global demand for accountability. The real impact happens on-chain, every single trade.
